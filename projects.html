"A Solution to Coastal Flooding in Irish Cities" - 2023

Description:

Investigated the effect of floods on Irish cities and residents, developing a new, community-friendly solution for flood defence infrastructure in the form of a motor-free retractable floodwall. Created a proposal for protecting seaside communities in Ireland.
