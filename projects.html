"MakerTerminal" - 2024

Aug 2024 - Present

Other Contributors: Julian

Description:

A platform for identifying suitable funding opportunities for early stage students/founders/entrepreneurs/hackers with limited resources. Users will be able to analyse & look through 4 key databases;

1) Investors (e.g VCs, individual Angels)

2) Grants (e.g EV, 1517, Bagel fund, Enterprise Ireland)

3) Accelerators & incubators (e.g NDRC, Antler, Entrepreneur First)

4) Competitions (e.g BTYS, Scifest, TECS)

The platform has filtering functionality to help these builders find the perfect funding sources or other opportunities for their particular projects based on location, amount, sector, stage, etc.

Launch date: 28th Nov
