"MakerTerminal" - Present

Aug 2024 - Present

Description:

Deck View

We are building a platform for identifying funding opportunities for early stage students/founders/entrepreneurs/hackers with limited resources. Users will be able to analyse & look through 4 key databases;

1) Investors (e.g VCs, individual Angels)

2) Grants (e.g EV, 1517, Bagel fund, Enterprise Ireland)

3) Accelerators & incubators (e.g NDRC, Antler, Entrepreneur First)

4) Competitions (e.g BTYS, Scifest, TECS)

The platform will have filtering functionality to help these builders find the perfect funding sources or other opportunities for their particular projects based on location, amount, sector, stage, etc.
