    My Ideas:

  1. Long term investment Simulation:


      Aim:   Give a user insight into the different possible returns their investment might have and the probability of each one using standard deviation of returns.
  2. Crypto Coin Investment Rating model:


      Aim:   Give user insight into the possible returns of a cryptocurrency investment, through looking at coin metrics known as ‘tokenomics’.
  3. Venture Capital startup Investment Rating model:


      Aim:   To allow Venture Capitalists to screen a startup through calculating their success probability before they agree to engage in a meeting/pitch.
  4. Investment Property Future Cashflow Model:


      Aim:   To allow landlords to calculate probability of different returns of an investment property in different economic climates.
      I then narrowed it down to 4 different types of probability simulations and scored each model on 5 different factors in a scoring matrix.

    Idea Scoring Matrix:

      From my above scoring matrix. I have chosen the investment simulation model over my other ideas for a few reasons; I find it most interesting. I also feel I can complete it in the given timeframe and be able to code it without extreme difficulty, but it should still challenge me. I also felt myself and others could find use in my model
